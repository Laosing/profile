About BracketSC2

BracketSC2 is a web application built to host and organize your Starcraft 2 tournaments. Set up tournaments with a map pool that gets constantly updated while also tagging players with their mastered race. The scores are edited real time and allows for constant updates. Host your tournaments now and find out the best player of them all!

Features

Simplified Tournament Management

Extremely simple tournament management. Keep track of all your tournaments that are completed or in progress. Update scores in real time and watch BracketSC2 run your tournament for you. There can only be one champion!

Built for Starcraft 2

This is Starcraft 2, the race that you’ve chosen to master should appear beside your player tag. Nothing screams domination better until you’ve destroyed every unbalanced race and map that gets thrown your way.
